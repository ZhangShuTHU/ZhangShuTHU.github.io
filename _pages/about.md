I develop integrated assessment and energy–economy–environment models to test how China can reach carbon neutrality under real constraints—from flexibility and resources to cost and uncertainty.

Trained across electrical engineering, economics, finance, and management, I connect system mechanics to policy choices. I earned my Ph.D. in Management from Tsinghua University and was a visiting research scholar at the International Institute for Applied Systems Analysis (IIASA) from 2022 to 2023.
